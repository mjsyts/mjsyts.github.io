Usage Guide

Primary: Ocean Blue (#43A0BD) remains your core brand color for logos, main navigation, and key brand moments.

Warm Accents: Use Warm Gold and Coral Sunset to create visual interest and draw attention to CTAs, featured content, or interactive elements.

Natural Balance: Leaf Green brings freshness and works well for success states, growth-oriented messaging, or environmental themes.

Cool Harmony: Aqua Teal and Deep Teal provide variation within the cool spectrum without feeling redundant.

Neutrals: Cream and Sky Tint give you warm and cool background options, while Forest Dark provides strong text contrast.
