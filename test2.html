/03 — About

I’m a composer and sound designer with a background in digital audio, DSP, and game-focused music research.

My work lives where careful craft meets strange, vivid worlds: layered textures, tight systems design, and sounds that support play without shouting over it.

I’m finishing a doctorate in music composition, with research on NES game audio and the ways technical limits shape musical imagination.

I love collaborating early with designers, writers, and programmers so audio can shape feel and pacing, not just decorate at the end.

If your project cares about mood, detail, and emotional clarity, I’d love to talk.
