About

I’m Josiah — a saxophonist, sound designer, composer, and DSP developer. My work lives at the intersection of musicianship and technical craft: instrument repair, audio tool development, and sustained work with sound as both material and system. The result is a practice grounded in how sound is made and attentive to how it behaves.

Composition, improvisation, sound design, and custom DSP are central to my practice. I currently live in Atlanta, GA and am completing a doctorate in music composition at Johns Hopkins.
